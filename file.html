Cardio Expert

Summary

Cardio Xpert is an advanced heart prediction platform designed to assess heart health and suggest preventive measures through two key features: "Take a Quiz" and "Chat with Chatbot". In the "Take a Quiz", users answer a series of heart health-related questions, allowing the system to analyze their cardiovascular condition. Based on the input, users receive a heart health percentage, risk assessment, and personalized precautions to maintain a healthy lifestyle. The "Chatbot" features serve as an AI-powered virtual assistant for emergencies or general heart health queries. Users can describe their symptoms, and the chatbot will provide immediate guidance, precautionary steps, and first-aid-based recommendations to manage the situation until professional medical help is available. Cardio Xpert empowers individuals to take charge of their heart health, ensuring early detection, timely intervention, and proactive wellness management.
